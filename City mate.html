How It Works

About

Welcome to City Mate, a smart and easy-to-use chatbot designed to help people explore cities and discover exciting places. Whether you’re a tourist visiting a new city or a resident looking for something fun to do, this assistant is here to guide you.

The chatbot asks a few quick questions to understand who you are and what you like. Based on your answers, it suggests places such as cozy cafes, parks, historical spots, or entertainment venues — all selected to match your preferences. The goal of this project is to make local travel planning simple, smart, and personalized for everyone.

Built with accessibility in mind, the chatbot runs directly in your browser without needing any downloads or signups. Just answer a few questions, and let the assistant take care of the rest!

Features

  • User-Type Based Suggestions: It asks whether you're a tourist or a local resident to better understand your needs.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Based on your interests, it suggests relevant places like cafes, parks, or historical sites.
  • Simple Chat Interface: A clean and easy-to-use interface that works like a conversation - no confusion or long forms.
  • Fast and Lightweight: It’s built to be simple and fast, so you get quick results without loading extra pages.
  • No Login Needed: You don’t need to create an account or enter personal details to use it - just click and go.
